Age Suitability for the Word Guessing Game

This document addresses the **age suitability** of words used within this guessing game.

The words permitted in this game generally adhere to the following rules:

  • They are **common nouns in their singular form**.
  • They only contain **letters from A to Z** (no umlauts or special characters).
  • They **do not contain any duplicate letters**.

However, please be aware that despite these guidelines, **some words encountered during gameplay or within the game's features may be ambiguous or more appropriate for adult players.**

Players may encounter such words in various scenarios, for instance:

  • An opponent player may make a guess that includes a word fitting this description.
  • An opponent player may choose such a word as their secret word.
  • In the **"dictionary" section**, such words may be presented as "words of the day" (five random words from the dictionary, renewed daily).
  • In the **"dictionary" section**, such words may appear as "proposals" where manually curated words are suggested and made available for voting.

We advise players to exercise discretion and be aware of this possibility during gameplay and when exploring game features.
